Decision Graveyard
Every choice you've ever made is buried here — the wins, the regrets, and everything in between.

Most people make a decision and move on. They never look back. They never ask why it worked, or why it didn't. Over time the pattern is lost — and they keep making the same mistakes, dressed in different clothes.

This is your archive of consequence. Log what you decided, why you decided it, and what came of it. Then revisit. Let the record speak. Let the patterns teach you something about yourself that no one else can.

Not a journal. Not a to-do list. A graveyard for choices — where every headstone tells a story worth reading.
